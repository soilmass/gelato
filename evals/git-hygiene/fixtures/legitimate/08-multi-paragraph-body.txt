feat(eval-harness): stream large fixture sets instead of buffering

The previous loadFixtures implementation read every fixture into
memory before handing them to the classifier. For skills with 100+
fixtures the peak RSS spiked over 500 MB and made CI brittle on
the 2-core runners.

The new implementation yields fixtures one at a time via an async
iterator. Classifiers don't need all fixtures present at once for
their shape checks, so throughput is unchanged and memory stays
well under 100 MB.
